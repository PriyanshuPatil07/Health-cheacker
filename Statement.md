Problem Statement

People often experience common symptoms such as mild fever, cough, headache, or tiredness and may want a simple way to check and understand their condition. However, they may not always know how to interpret these symptoms. The purpose of this project is to create a basic, console-based Python program that asks the user a few simple health-related questions and then provides a clear summary of their symptoms along with general, non-medical comfort tips such as resting, staying hydrated, or taking warm fluids.
This program is designed for educational use only, helping users become more aware of their symptoms while practicing basic Python programming concepts.
